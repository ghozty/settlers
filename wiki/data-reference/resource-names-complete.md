## Important Notes

### Coin Variants
- `Coin` - Star Menu items (Add Resource)
- `Coins` - Alternative
- `Money` - Warehouse/storage

### Singular/Plural Forms
- `Tool` / `Tools`
- `Gem` / `Gems`
- `StarCoin` / `StarCoins`
- `GuildCoin` / `GuildCoins`

- `SaddleCloth` / `Saddlecloth`
- `MagicBeanStalk` / `MagicBeanstalk`
- `Brew` / `Beer`
